Introduction

Stink bugs have become a major threat to profitable cotton production in the Southeast. This is primarily from the low spray environment resulting from the widespread adoption of Bt cottons.

The use of a simple decision aid can help producers and scouts:

  • select the correct boll sizes for stink bug damage assessments,

  • recognize stink bug damage,

  • follow straightforward scouting steps, and

  • utilize the “dynamic” threshold to avoid yield losses and minimize unnecessary insecticide use.
