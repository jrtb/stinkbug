Stink Bug Scouting Steps
• Pull a random sample of quarter size diameter bolls, avoiding field edges. (boll sizes between 0.9 inches and 1.1 inches)
• 1 boll / acre, no less than 25 / field.
   
• Sort bolls into two piles: those with and those without obvious external lesions.
   
• Crack open and and inspect bolls with external lesions for internal damage (bolls with warts, stained seed or stained lint).
   
• If threshold is not met for that week (see dynamic threshold), check the remaining bolls for internal damage.
   
• Treat field only if the threshold is met for that week.
