Summary

A pocket-size scouting decision aid was developed for use in the Southeast to encourage (1) enhanced adoption of stink bug scouting in cotton, (2) better field identification of stink bug-induced boll damage symptoms, and (3) use of recommended scouting procedures.

This stink bug decision aid and associated information provides the end user with the information needed to efficiently scout for stink bug damage to quarter-sized bolls, minimizing both boll damage and insecticide.
