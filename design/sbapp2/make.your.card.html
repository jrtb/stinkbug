Make Your Own Field Decision Aid Card:

 

1) Click on this MS Word template (or PDF) which has the image of the card sized to 6 x 6 inches. Or you can download this field card app image to print out in another program.

2) print out the image on label paper, (ie Avery 5265)

3) cut out a 3x6 inch piece of Plexiglas or plywood

4) cut and affix the label to each side of the Plexiglas (with the holes oriented correctly)

5) cut the holes on the field card with a drill press using a 7/8 or 15/16 inch drill bit for the small hole & a 1-1/16 or 1-1/8 drill bit for the larger hole.
